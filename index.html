How to take care the plants

1. Watering

  • * Understand each plant's water needs: Some plants require frequent watering, while others prefer drier conditions.

2. Light
  • * Understand each plant's light needs: To grow up and get more energy.
3. Temperature and Humidity:
  • * Maintain suitable temperature: Most plants have temperature preferences. Avoid placing them in drafty or extreme temperature conditions.
4. Fertilizing:
  • *Use appropriate fertilizers: Different plants may require different types of fertilizers (e.g., liquid, slow-release). Follow instructions on the fertilizer package for application.
